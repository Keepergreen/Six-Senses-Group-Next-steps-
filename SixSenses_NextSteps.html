06 — What Success Looks Like

Outcomes that matter at both levels

Asset Diversion Rate
Total assets diverted from disposal as a % of eligible items — documented per property and in aggregate across the Six Senses portfolio.
CO₂e Avoided
Scope 3 avoided emissions across Cat. 2, 5 & 12 — calculated to DEFRA / WRAP CarbonWARM2 methodology, audit-ready and GSTC/GRI-aligned.
Financial Value Recovered
Revenue and in-kind donation value documented per asset category — building the owner ROI case for ongoing operations investment and HMA retention.
Social Impact Verified
Downstream beneficiaries, organisations, and communities supported — documented with verified partner data and first-person testimony, not estimates.
Staff Engagement
Measurable staff participation in the asset redirection process — a visible contribution to Six Senses' sustainability culture and an HR and marketing asset at property level.
Group-Level Disclosure Output
All metrics consolidated into a single portfolio view — aligned to Six Senses' GSTC reporting, IHG group CSRD exposure, and GRI 306 waste disclosure.
For Six Senses, this programme represents the first time end-of-life asset management is measured, reported, and community-connected at both property and group level — turning a current gap in circular asset reporting into a leadership position consistent with how Six Senses has approached sustainability since its founding.
Beyond diversion and donation
Keeper creates a new layer of operational intelligence — helping Six Senses understand asset lifecycles, recovery potential, waste patterns, and circular value opportunities across the portfolio. Circular asset management in hotels is an unsolved problem globally. This is the opportunity to build the standard.
